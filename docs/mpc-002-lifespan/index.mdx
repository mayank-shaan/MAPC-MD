# MPC-002: Life Span Psychology

## Course Overview

This course examines human development across the entire lifespan, from conception to death. It explores physical, cognitive, social, and emotional changes that occur throughout life.

## Course Structure

### Block 1: Introduction & Methods
- Unit 1: Introduction to Life Span Development
- Unit 2: Research Methods in Developmental Psychology
- Unit 3: Theoretical Perspectives
- Unit 4: Biological Foundations

### Block 2: Early Development
- Unit 1: Prenatal Development & Birth
- Unit 2: Infancy & Toddlerhood
- Unit 3: Early Childhood
- Unit 4: Middle Childhood

### Block 3: Adolescence & Adulthood
- Unit 1: Adolescent Development
- Unit 2: Early Adulthood
- Unit 3: Middle Adulthood
- Unit 4: Relationships & Family

### Block 4: Aging & Death
- Unit 1: Late Adulthood
- Unit 2: Cognitive Aging
- Unit 3: Death, Dying & Bereavement
- Unit 4: Successful Aging
