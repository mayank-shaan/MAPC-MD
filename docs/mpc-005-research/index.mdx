# MPC-005: Research Methods in Psychology

## Course Overview

This course provides comprehensive training in psychological research methods. It covers research design, data collection, ethical considerations, and scientific writing.

## Course Structure

### Block 1: Foundations of Research
- Unit 1: Introduction to Research Methods
- Unit 2: Scientific Method in Psychology
- Unit 3: Research Ethics
- Unit 4: Literature Review

### Block 2: Research Designs
- Unit 1: Experimental Designs
- Unit 2: Quasi-Experimental Designs
- Unit 3: Correlational Research
- Unit 4: Survey Methods

### Block 3: Qualitative Methods
- Unit 1: Introduction to Qualitative Research
- Unit 2: Interviews & Focus Groups
- Unit 3: Observation Methods
- Unit 4: Content Analysis

### Block 4: Data & Reporting
- Unit 1: Sampling Techniques
- Unit 2: Measurement & Scales
- Unit 3: Writing Research Reports
- Unit 4: APA Style & Publication
