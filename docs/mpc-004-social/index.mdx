# MPC-004: Social Psychology

## Course Overview

This course explores how individuals think, feel, and behave in social contexts. It examines social influence, group dynamics, attitudes, and interpersonal relationships.

## Course Structure

### Block 1: Foundations
- Unit 1: Introduction to Social Psychology
- Unit 2: Research Methods in Social Psychology
- Unit 3: Social Cognition
- Unit 4: Social Perception

### Block 2: Social Influence
- Unit 1: Attitudes & Attitude Change
- Unit 2: Conformity & Compliance
- Unit 3: Obedience & Authority
- Unit 4: Persuasion Techniques

### Block 3: Group Processes
- Unit 1: Group Dynamics
- Unit 2: Leadership & Power
- Unit 3: Intergroup Relations
- Unit 4: Prejudice & Discrimination

### Block 4: Interpersonal Relations
- Unit 1: Attraction & Relationships
- Unit 2: Prosocial Behavior
- Unit 3: Aggression
- Unit 4: Applied Social Psychology
